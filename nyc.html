my favorite spots
♥
The Ramble, Central Park
Upper West Side
Go deep into The Ramble, not the main mall. Migratory birds stop here in spring. You'll forget Manhattan exists for exactly 40 minutes.
♥
The High Line at dawn
Chelsea / Meatpacking
An elevated rail track turned garden. Genuinely lovely before the tourists arrive. The wildflower sections in May are perfect.
♥
Blank Street Coffee
Lower East Side
Tiny, efficient, and somehow always perfectly lit for your notes. The oat matcha latte is genuinely good.
♥
City Reliquary
Williamsburg, Brooklyn
A tiny museum of random NYC artifacts — old subway tokens, Statue of Liberty fragments. $7 and 45 minutes will leave you oddly emotional.
♥
Governors Island
New York Harbor
Take the free ferry on a weekday. The best view of lower Manhattan, almost no one there, and you can rent a bike and feel like you left the city entirely.
